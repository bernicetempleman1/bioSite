Welcome to Bernice Templeman’s bioSite.

Bernice Templeman is a software developer with over 30 years of experience in Information Technology (IT). She has worked as a ServiceNow Developer, Linux and Unix System Administrator, VMware administrator, Network Attached Storage (NAS) technical support engineer II, and software engineer. In her previous work, she wrote code using JavaScript on the ServiceNow Platform as a Service (PaaS); leveraged familiarity with CSS and HTML to ensure seamless front-end content delivery.

Bernice has a Master of Science in Information Systems (MS in CIS) and a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (BS in CS) . Bernice is currently updating her skillset by completing a second Bachelor of Science in Web Development. Bernice is certified in ServiceNow system administration (CSA), application development (CAD), IT Service Management (ITSM), Human Resources Service Delivery (HRSD), Performance Analytics (PA), and Event Management (EM).

Bernice is a lifelong learner which is needed to stay up to date with technology.

In her free time, Bernice enjoys exercising, walking, cooking and eating healthy at home.
